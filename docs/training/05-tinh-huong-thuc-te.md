## 🛒 Scenario 1: Startup E-commerce "Phở Sài Gòn Online"

### 🎯 **Bối Cảnh**
**Khách hàng**: Anh Minh Tuấn, 35 tuổi, chủ quán phở gia đình tại Q1, TP.HCM
**Vấn đề**: Doanh thu giảm 40% do COVID, muốn chuyển online
**Mục tiêu**: Xây dựng platform delivery, tăng doanh thu 200% trong 6 tháng

### 📋 **Requirements Ban Đầu**
```
🎯 BUSINESS GOALS:
• Bán phở online delivery
• Phục vụ 100 đơn/ngày
• Mở rộng 3km radius
• Build brand "Phở Sài Gòn Authentic"

💻 TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
• Website responsive 
• Mobile app (phase 2)
• Payment: VNPAY, MoMo, COD
• Delivery tracking
• Inventory management

👥 TARGET USERS:
• Office workers (11AM-2PM)
• Families (6PM-8PM) 
• Age: 25-45
• Income: 15M+ VNĐ/tháng
```

### 🚀 **30-Day Development Timeline**

#### **Week 1: Analysis & Strategy (Days 1-7)**

##### Day 1-2: Business Analysis Agent
```
📊 INPUT:
• Business info: Family restaurant, 10 years experience
• Current revenue: 50M VNĐ/month offline
• Location: District 1, HCMC
• Menu: 8 phở varieties + appetizers
• Staff: 5 people

🤖 AI ANALYSIS OUTPUT:
• Market size: 2.3B USD Vietnam food delivery
• Direct competitors: Phở 24, Phở Ông Hùng
• Indirect: Grab Food, Baemin merchants
• Opportunity: Authentic North-style phở niche
• Price recommendation: 45,000-65,000 VNĐ/bowl
• Peak hours: 11:30AM-1:30PM, 6:30PM-8:30PM
```

##### Day 3-4: Strategy Agent
```
💡 BUSINESS MODEL:
• B2C direct delivery platform
• Commission: 15% (vs 30% Grab Food)
• Free delivery >100K VNĐ
• Loyalty program: 10th bowl free

📈 FINANCIAL PROJECTIONS:
Month 1: 600 orders → 35M VNĐ revenue
Month 3: 1,500 orders → 85M VNĐ revenue  
Month 6: 3,000 orders → 170M VNĐ revenue
Break-even: Month 2
ROI: 250% first year

🎯 MARKETING STRATEGY:
• Facebook Ads (40% budget)
• Google Ads (30% budget)  
• Influencer partnerships (20% budget)
• Referral program (10% budget)
```
