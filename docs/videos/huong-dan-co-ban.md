## 📊 **Video Production Guidelines**

### 🎥 **Technical Standards**

#### Recording Specifications
```
📹 VIDEO QUALITY:
- Resolution: 1920x1080 minimum
- Frame rate: 30fps consistent
- Bitrate: 8-12 Mbps for quality
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)

🎤 AUDIO REQUIREMENTS:
- Sample rate: 48kHz
- Bitrate: 192kbps minimum
- Format: AAC stereo
- Noise floor: -60dB minimum
- Room tone: Record 30 seconds
```

#### Editing Standards
```
✂️ POST-PRODUCTION:
- Color correction: Consistent brightness/contrast
- Audio normalization: -16 LUFS target
- Transitions: Simple cuts, minimal effects
- Graphics: On-brand colors and fonts
- Captions: Vietnamese subtitles always

📱 EXPORT SETTINGS:
- YouTube: 1080p, 30fps, VBR 8-12 Mbps
- Social media: Additional 720p, vertical formats
- Website: Compressed versions for faster loading
```
